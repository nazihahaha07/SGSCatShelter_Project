About Us

Welcome to SGS Cat Shelter

We are dedicated to providing a safe and loving environment for cats in need. SGS shelter offers a temporary home for cats until they find their forever families. We believe that every cat deserves a chance at a happy and healthy life, and we work tirelessly to ensure they receive the care they need.

Our team of volunteers is passionate about animal welfare and works hard to make sure every cat is well taken care of. From providing medical care to organizing adoption events, we are committed to the well-being of each and every cat that comes through our doors.
